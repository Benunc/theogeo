Russia & Central Asia — Ch. 15–16

Study plan → flashcards → quiz. Open this file in any browser (double-click index.html).

3-day study plan (~30 min/day)

  1. Day 1 — Regions & vocabulary
    Read the four regions and their traits until you can name one fact about each. Then drill vocab: Chernozem, continentality, taiga, Eurasia, Siberia, Central Asia, yurt, nomad, supra.
  2. Day 2 — History & economies
    Czar / Peter the Great, Russian Revolution, USSR, command economy vs. capitalism, collective farms, Cold War, Great Game, Baltic republics. Say aloud: What happened to farmers on collective farms?
  3. Day 3 — Places & environment
    Lake Baikal, Aral Sea, Silk Roads, Urals, Volga, Caspian, Northern European Plain, Trans-Siberian Railroad, General Winter, Land of Flames, nuclear testing, dachas, banyas. Finish with a full Quiz tab run; repeat wrong answers.

Tip: Parent asks “Why?” follow-ups (e.g. Why is the Aral Sea hard to restore?) — one sentence answers are enough.
